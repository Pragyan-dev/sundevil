Getting Ready to Graduate

Congratulations on your upcoming graduation! Be sure to complete the tasks listed below, so you're ready for the big day.

  • Purchase graduation regalia online through Herff Jones until April 21 or in person at Sun Devil Campus Stores through graduation. Check store hours.
  • RSVP for commencement and convocation ceremonies by clicking on the link available under the My Programs Graduation tab (below).
  • Confirm the Diploma Address listed on your My ASU Profile tab is correct. Your diploma will be sent to the diploma address listed.
  • Review your full name on your My ASU Profile tab. This is how your name will display on your diploma. If you want to change how your name will display, submit a Diploma Name form.
  • Learn more about applying for graduation on the Apply for Graduation page.

A Barrett application cannot be viewed by the college until it has been finished in its entirety, saved, and submitted via the "Submit Application" button. A student will not be able to make any changes to his or her application once it is submitted.
Admitted to ASU

A Barrett application may be started and submitted before ASU admission is conferred. However, it cannot go to evaluation until university acceptance is formally awarded.
ACT or SAT Scores

All Barrett applicants must have official ACT or SAT scores on file with ASU Admission Services. Test scores should not be submitted to Barrett unless specifically requested.
